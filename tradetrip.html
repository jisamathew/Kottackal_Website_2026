Outcome

Execute global trade without manual coordination

Cross-border trade today is fragmented, paper-based, and opaque. TradeTrip automates the full trade lifecycle — from order placement to final fund settlement — while ensuring every step is verifiable and auditable on the blockchain.

What TradeTrip Enables

Intelligent coordination across every trade stakeholder

Four core capabilities that transform how cross-border trade operates.

Autonomous Workflow Execution

End-to-end automation of the full trade lifecycle — LC issuance, Bill of Lading, packing lists, and fund settlement — without manual coordination.

Verifiable Systems & Auditability

Every action immutably recorded on Ethereum. Every document has cryptographic proof. Every party verified through GLEIF LEI.

Ecosystem Integration

Banks, sellers, buyers, shippers, and port authorities coordinate through one platform — replacing fragmented systems with a unified operational view.

Secure Data Collaboration

Trade documents stored on IPFS — decentralised, tamper-proof, and always accessible. Data shared across borders without compromising integrity.
