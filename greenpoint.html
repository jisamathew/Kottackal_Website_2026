Platform Capabilities

What GreenPoint enables

Six core capabilities that transform how energy-mobility operations are managed, optimised, and planned.

Unified Data Aggregation

A single source of truth pulling from any connected asset — regardless of brand or provider — into one intuitive dashboard. No more multi-platform management.

Advanced Data Fusion

Proprietary algorithms intelligently fuse vehicle telematics with charging history, on-site energy generation, and real-time weather data — creating the foundation for true intelligence.

Digital Twin Technology

Create virtual models of vehicles, fleets, or entire facilities. Run simulations to predict battery performance, model fleet electrification, and test operational scenarios before deployment.

Energy Optimisation

Simulate charging at different times of day using time-of-use electricity rates. Identify the most cost-effective schedule for every vehicle and reduce energy costs by up to 15%.

Battery Performance Prediction

Model the impact of temperature, driving behaviour, and charging patterns on battery degradation — enabling proactive maintenance and eliminating range anxiety.

Hardware Agnosticism

Works with any EV brand, charger network, or energy provider. No vendor lock-in. GreenPoint integrates with what you already have and what you’ll add next.

Use Case

Optimising an electrified logistics fleet

A major logistics company electrifying its delivery fleet used vehicles from three manufacturers, charging across two public networks and a private depot.

Before GreenPoint

The fleet manager spent hours manually reconciling charging reports with vehicle mileage — incomplete data and guesswork. Unable to optimise charging schedules. Worried about unpredictable battery performance on cold days.

With GreenPoint

All vehicles and chargers connected to one platform. Real-time telematics fused with charging data and weather. Simulations model cold-snap impact on range and recommend optimal routes and charging schedules. Result: 15% energy cost reduction, zero range anxiety.
