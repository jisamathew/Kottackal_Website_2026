ZK-VAIAP — Platform Overview
What We Solve

Cryptographic proof that your AI is safe and compliant

Current AI governance relies on documentation and post-hoc audits — approaches that will fail as AI systems grow more complex. Regulators and hospitals need verifiable proof, not trust-based assurances.

ZK-VAIAP provides that proof using zero-knowledge cryptography — without exposing sensitive patient data or proprietary model internals.

The Problem & Solution

AI governance built on trust — and why it breaks

The Problem Today
01
Unverifiable privacy claims
Hospitals and regulators cannot independently verify whether patient data (PII) is truly removed before AI processing.
02
Trust-based audits don’t scale
Documentation and post-hoc reviews are insufficient as AI systems grow more complex and powerful in regulated sectors.
03
Slow AI adoption & compliance risk
Lack of verifiable assurance blocks AI deployment in healthcare, creating regulatory penalties and reputational risk.
The ZK-VAIAP Solution
01
Cryptographic proof, not documentation
Zero-knowledge proofs verify that privacy, safety, and fairness requirements are met — without revealing data or models.
02
Continuous compliance monitoring
Always-on verification throughout the AI system’s lifecycle — ready for regulatory review at any moment.
03
Detect hidden risks in AI models
Cryptographic analysis finds bias, data leakage, and vulnerabilities that surface-level documentation audits miss entirely.
Platform Capabilities

Verifiable AI assurance, end to end

Purpose-built for regulated sectors where AI compliance must be provable, not assumed.

Zero-Knowledge Proof Engine

Provides cryptographic proof that AI systems meet privacy, safety, and fairness requirements — without revealing sensitive data or proprietary model internals.

PII Verification

Verifiable proof that personally identifiable information is fully removed before AI processing — critical for healthcare, finance, and other regulated sectors.

Hidden Risk Detection

Detects risks that surface-level audits miss — bias, data leakage, model vulnerabilities — using cryptographic analysis rather than documentation review.

Continuous Monitoring

Ongoing verification that compliance is maintained throughout the AI system’s lifecycle — not just at point of deployment, but continuously in production.

Regulatory Audit Readiness

Always-on audit trail and compliance evidence for regulators — accelerating approvals and reducing the cost of regulatory engagement.
