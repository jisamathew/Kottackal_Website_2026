The Problem

Infrastructure fails because monitoring is reactive

Critical infrastructure, energy systems, and urban utilities face fragmented monitoring — detecting issues only after they occur, at massive cost.

The Problem Today
01
Monitoring is fragmented & reactive
Sensor data is siloed across systems with no real-time view — issues only surface after they cause costly outages or failures.
02
Unplanned failures drain budgets
Calendar-driven maintenance and unexpected breakdowns inflate operational costs and shorten the lifespan of critical assets.
03
No geospatial operational picture
Asset health cannot be mapped to location, making infrastructure planning and cross-site risk assessment largely guesswork.
Our Solution
01
Predict faults before they occur
Quantum ML fuses classical and virtual sensor data to surface anomalies early — giving operators time to act before any failure.
02
Condition-based maintenance
Maintenance schedules driven by real asset condition — not calendars — reducing reactive interventions and O&M costs.
03
Unified geospatial intelligence
Asset health, anomalies, and predictions mapped across geographic locations and integrated with existing control systems.
